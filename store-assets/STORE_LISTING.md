## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Sharpen your mind with 200IQ Brain Training - the ultimate brain workout app featuring 4 addictive game modes designed to boost your cognitive abilities!

GAME MODES:

Speed Math - Race against the clock solving arithmetic problems. How fast can your brain calculate?

Pattern Match - Identify number sequences and logical patterns. Train your analytical thinking!

Memory Grid - Test and improve your visual memory with an increasingly challenging grid. Remember which cells light up!

Color Match - The classic Stroop test challenges your cognitive flexibility. What COLOR is the text, not what it says!

FEATURES:
- 30-second fast-paced rounds perfect for quick brain breaks
- Daily streak tracking to build your training habit
- Detailed statistics and game history
- Score grading system (S, A, B, C, D) to track improvement
- Weekly leaderboard to compete with players worldwide
- Daily Challenge mode combining all 4 games
- Share your scores with friends
- Beautiful dark theme that's easy on the eyes
- 100% free to play

WHY BRAIN TRAINING?
Studies show that regular cognitive exercises can help maintain mental sharpness. Just 5 minutes a day can make a difference. 200IQ Brain Training makes it fun and addictive!

Perfect for:
- Students preparing for exams
- Professionals wanting to stay sharp
- Anyone who enjoys puzzles and brain teasers
- People looking for a productive way to pass time
